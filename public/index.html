The Game

A whole world to build, and to keep.

Nature & Nations is a planet-scale city-builder and nature simulation. You found and grow communities — from a single hamlet to a nation — on a faithful model of the real Earth, where weather, water, ecology, and people all run on real forces.

It blends three games into one: the joy of a city-builder, the depth of a grand-strategy nation simulation, and the soul of a living ecology sim. Every choice ripples outward — from one street to the whole world — and the world remembers. Build well, tend faithfully, and watch a civilization rise; neglect it, and watch it groan. The world is alive whether you are watching or not.

What Makes It Different

Every life leaves a legacy.

Most city-builders forget. Nature & Nations remembers — people, choices, and consequences carry forward across generations, so every world grows a history that is truly its own.

Consequences emerge — they aren’t scripted

You never click “crime +4%.” Underfund schools for a decade and watch it ripple outward — unemployment, poverty, blight, migration — a chain you can trace but never fully predict. The world reacts the way the real one does: through cause and effect.

Your citizens are people, with families

They are born, raise children, and pass their traits, wealth, and reputation down the line. A founder’s great-granddaughter becomes governor; a crime boss’s bloodline endures until your policies break the cycle. Dynasties, companies, and rivalries persist for centuries.

Your city writes its own history

Headlines, milestones, and lives are recorded as they happen — so a hundred years on you don’t just remember buildings. You remember families, founders, scandals, and the eras that shaped the skyline. Each playthrough becomes a civilization you can tell stories about.

A Living Earth

The world is not a backdrop. It was here before you.

A whole planet, modeled on the real Earth — its mountains, coasts, climate, and sky, running on real forces rather than stagecraft.

Planet Scale

The whole Earth, from orbit to underfoot

Stand on real geography drawn from true elevation data, and rise from street level to the curve of the planet in one continuous sweep. Sculpt and shape the land — it remembers.

Weather & Seasons

A true global climate

Wind, pressure, and temperature flow from real atmospheric circulation. Storms form and travel; seasons turn with the tilt of the world; deserts, monsoons, and snow belong where they would on Earth.

Water & Sky

Rivers carved by the land, skies that turn

Water gathers where real elevation sends it — rivers run downhill, lakes fill basins. A real sun crosses the sky, clouds drift with the wind, and on the right nights the aurora answers to real space weather.

Living Ecology

Forests, grasslands, and the breath of the wind

Vegetation belongs to its biome, swaying in the very wind the weather drives. The living world fills the land you tend, and flourishes or fades by how you keep it.

Nature’s Fury

A world that can turn on you

The same living Earth that gives can also take. Real natural disasters strike where they would on the real planet — so a wise steward builds for them. They’re conveyed through warnings, stats, and overlays, never graphic harm.

The People

Every person is a soul, not a statistic.

The people who fill your world are born, grow, age, and pass. They carry families, faces, faith, and frailty — and they respond to how they are treated.

Families & Lineage

Households form; bloodlines run deep

People pair, raise families, and pass their traits to their children. Follow a lineage across generations — the story of a place is the story of its families.

Faces & Bodies

No two people alike

Every person has a distinct, inherited face and body across the full range of real human variation. The crowd you walk through is genuinely a crowd of individuals.

Culture & Belief

Communities with their own way of life

Regions carry their own faiths, customs, and dress, blending across borders the way real cultures do. What a people values shapes how they live — and how they respond to you.

Mind & Wellbeing

People flourish — or they suffer

Crowding, neglect, and hardship weigh on people; safety, beauty, community, and care lift them. A city that looks prosperous can still be quietly breaking its people — and you will see it.

Questions

Frequently asked

What is Nature & Nations?
A planet-scale city-builder and nature simulation. You raise civilizations on a living, real-world Earth and tend a world that responds to every choice you make.
What kind of game is it?
It blends a city-builder, a grand-strategy nation simulation, and a living ecology sim, built on Unreal Engine 5.8.
When does it release?
Nature & Nations is coming in 2026. Wishlist it on Steam to be notified the moment it launches.
What platforms will it be on?
It launches on PC (Windows) via Steam, with macOS support after launch and Linux / Steam Deck planned.
Is it single-player?
Yes. Play Greenfield mode, building the only civilization on an untouched world, or Living Nations mode in a world of rival AI nations that trade, scheme, and war.
Does it use real Earth geography?
Yes. The world is modeled on the real Earth, with true terrain, climate, weather, and biomes from pole to equator.
How much will it cost?
Pricing will be announced closer to launch. Wishlist on Steam to catch the announcement.
Is more mature content or DLC planned?
The base game is rated Teen and complete on its own. After launch, an optional, age-gated Realism expansion (ESRB Mature, 17+) will add the harder dimensions of civilization for players who want them — the same systemic honesty, applied to weightier subjects. It’s a deeper layer of the same world, not a separate game.
